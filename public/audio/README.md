# Ambient music

Drop your soundtrack here as **`ambient.mp3`**:

```
public/audio/ambient.mp3
```

It begins playing (fading in gently) the moment a guest taps to open the
envelope, with a floating mute toggle in the bottom-right corner.

If no file is present, the site works normally — the mute toggle simply
hides itself.

## Suggested vibe
A soft, looping instrumental — sitar, santoor, light piano, or a gentle
ambient pad. Keep it under ~2–3 MB and seamless to loop.

Royalty-free sources: Pixabay Music, Uppbeat, YouTube Audio Library.
